## What Causes the "ACTIVATE DISPLAYNAME FIELD MISSING FROM REGISTRY" Error?
 
The "ACTIVATE DISPLAYNAME FIELD MISSING FROM REGISTRY" error occurs when Windows 10 cannot find the product key or digital license associated with your device. This can happen for various reasons, such as:
 
- You have upgraded from a previous version of Windows that was not activated.
- You have changed your motherboard or other hardware components that affect your device ID.
- You have installed a wrong or corrupted version of Windows 10.
- You have modified or deleted some registry entries related to activation.

Whatever the reason, the result is that Windows 10 cannot verify your license and displays the error message "ACTIVATE DISPLAYNAME FIELD MISSING FROM REGISTRY" when you try to activate it.
 
## How to Fix the "ACTIVATE DISPLAYNAME FIELD MISSING FROM REGISTRY" Error?
 
Fortunately, there are some easy ways to fix this error and activate your Windows 10 license. Here are some possible solutions:
 
### Solution 1: Run the Activation Troubleshooter
 
The first thing you should try is to run the activation troubleshooter, which is a built-in tool that can detect and fix common activation problems. To do this, follow these steps:

1. Go to Settings > Update & Security > Activation.
2. Click on Troubleshoot under the activation status.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions and let the troubleshooter scan and fix any issues.
4. Restart your device and check if the error is resolved.

### Solution 2: Enter Your Product Key or Digital License
 
If the troubleshooter did not work, you can try to enter your product key or digital license manually. To do this, follow these steps:

1. Go to Settings > Update & Security > Activation.
2. Click on Change product key under the activation status.
3. Enter your 25-digit product key or your digital license if you have one. You can find your product key on a sticker on your device or in the confirmation email you received when you purchased Windows 10. If you don't have a product key or digital license, you can buy one from [Microsoft's website](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/get-windows-10).
4. Click on Next and follow the on-screen instructions to activate your Windows 10 license.
5. Restart your device and check if the error is resolved.
